>[!NOTE]+ Notes
>
>- "bh" and "mh" are usually pronounced as "v" or "w" (ва/вэ or вау/вэйл).
>- "ch" is a guttural "kh" (like Russian "х").
>- "dh" and "gh" are soft, like a voiced "kh" (sometimes like "y" or "g").
>- "ai" and "ae" are like "eye" (ай).
>- "ea" is like "a" in "cat" (э).
>- "oi" is like "oy" in "boy" (ой).
>- "ua" is like "oo-a" (уа).
>- "á" is a long "aw" (а).
